We believe in the power of tutoring because of the focus and attention the tutor is able to place on the student. A great tutor must not only know the material, but must also be an incredible communicator. That's why our goal is to communicate and foster growth in a manner that makes most sense for the student, and to do so with clarity, concern, and most importantly, encouragement.

We not only help students master material, but we equip them with the skills necessary for long term academic success. We instill in each student self-confidence and an intellectual curiosity that are the hallmarks of a good education. We help them uncover their own unlimited potential.



Luigi Patruno, Founder & Tutor

My passion is empowering students to see they are capable of learning anything. After studying mathematics and computer science at the undergraduate and graduate levels and working as a Data Scientist and Engineer, I decided to return to what I care most about, education. I've tutored students in math, science, and logic courses for over 12 years and I teach several graduate level courses in statistics and computer science at Fordham University in New York City.

If you are struggling with math or science and want to succeed, contact us. I look forward to learning with you!
